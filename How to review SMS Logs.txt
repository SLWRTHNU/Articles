 How to review SMS Logs
This guide provides a straightforward process for reviewing SMS logs, making it easy for users to access and analyze their messaging data. 

Click “Telephony”


Click “SMS Number”


Search for User or SMS number by entering the data in this field

Click the Telephone Number


Click the down arrow on the Menu Button


Click “SMS Logs”


Select the desired data range


Click “Generate”


Click “Export” to download the logs
