<p>
  If you've forgotten your <strong>Vanillasoft VoIP portal</strong> password or
  need to reset it for security reasons, you can quickly regain access using the
  password reset feature on the login page. This self-service option allows you
  to reset your password without contacting support, ensuring you can get back
  to work quickly. The reset process uses your registered email address or phone
  number to send you a secure link for creating a new password.
</p>
<p>
  Password resets are processed immediately, and the reset link is valid for a
  limited time. If you encounter issues during the reset process, multiple failed
  login attempts will temporarily lock your account for security purposes.
</p>
<hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
<div class="accordion">
  <p>
    <strong>Reset Your Password</strong>
  </p>
</div>
<div class="panel">
  <ol>
    <li data-list-item-id="ec1215261d13e9e37d114b6e3e2b6cd4f">
      <p>
        Navigate to the
        <a href="https://connect.cloudli.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Vanillasoft VoIP portal</strong></a>
        login page and select <strong>Forgot Your Password</strong>.<br>
        <img src="/guide-media/01KHC77TYBJ37HBXYKFJJGS8VH" width="502" height="202">
      </p>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
    </li>
    <li data-list-item-id="ec9629299a6e439048a80c6fcd251b077">
      <p>
        Enter the email address or phone number associated with your account.<br>
        <img src="/guide-media/01KHC7CXTQ5A73XFVAF9Q292QE" width="502" height="202">
      </p>
      <ul>
        <li data-list-item-id="e8364bd06b2559039e3f85fe35bfd74c9">
          <p>
            <strong>Email address:</strong> Enter your work email address
            that is registered to your account.&nbsp;
          </p>
          <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
        </li>
        <li data-list-item-id="e84b84adb4902f48e475b20759e30493a">
          <strong>Phone number:</strong> If you use your phone number to
          log in, enter your 10-digit phone number in the following format:
          <strong>XXXXXXXXXX</strong> (e.g., 5141112345).
        </li>
      </ul>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
    </li>
    <li data-list-item-id="ecc49b75c2eb5af1dd015e7905248ffab">
      <p>
        Click <strong>Next</strong>.<br>
        <img src="/guide-media/01KHC7GFTGEPH3KTVSA9R5JF28" width="502" height="202">
      </p>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
    </li>
  </ol>
  <p>
    You will receive a notification confirming that a password reset link has
    been emailed to you. Check your email inbox (and spam or junk folder if needed)
    for the password reset message. Click the link provided in the email to proceed
    with resetting your password.
  </p>
  <p>
    When you open the password reset link, you'll be prompted to create a new
    password. Your new password must meet the following requirements:
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li data-list-item-id="e8ab7652d80dac084b600ae4dd74e3ac7">
      <p>
        Your password must contain at least eight (8) characters.&nbsp;
      </p>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
    </li>
    <li data-list-item-id="e41b005b55687efd1204fc4391ffee17e">
      <p>The password is case sensitive.&nbsp;</p>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
    </li>
    <li data-list-item-id="e67b0ec9d70c5522343184a727410cb4c">
      Ensure your Caps Lock key is off when entering your password.
    </li>
  </ul>
  <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
  <p>
    After entering and confirming your new password, click <strong>Save</strong>
    or <strong>Submit</strong> to complete the password reset process. You can
    then log in to the Vanillasoft VoIP portal using your new credentials.
  </p>
  <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
</div>

    <strong>Troubleshooting</strong>
  </p>
</div>
<div class="panel">
  <p>
    If you encounter issues while resetting your password, the following tips
    may help:
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li data-list-item-id="e67afe0626cfb2de2ec313a91df4b07f6">
      <p>
        <strong>Account Lockout:</strong> Multiple failed login attempts
        will lock your account for 1 hour as a security measure. Wait for
        the lockout period to expire, or contact
        <a href="https://support.vanillasoft.com/hc/en-us/requests/new" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Vanillasoft Support</a>
        if you need immediate access.&nbsp;
      </p>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
    </li>
    <li data-list-item-id="e27fb5552f07501c154f3a41d873b9ca1">
      <p>
        <strong>Phone Number Format:</strong> If your identifier is a phone
        number, it must be entered in the format <strong>XXXXXXXXXX</strong>
        (e.g., 5141112345) without dashes, spaces, or parentheses.&nbsp;
      </p>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
    </li>
    <li data-list-item-id="e594e7bf5268538d5546588aaa4739592">
      <p>
        <strong>Email Not Received:</strong> Check your spam or junk folder
        for the password reset email. If you still don't see it, verify that
        you entered the correct email address and try again.&nbsp;
      </p>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
    </li>
    <li data-list-item-id="e68a161af03d8c0816757c34f1e0d5268">
      <strong>Expired Reset Link:</strong> Password reset links are valid for
      a limited time. If your link has expired, return to the login page and
      request a new password reset.
    </li>
  </ul>
