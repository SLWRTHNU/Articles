<p>
  The <strong>Vanillasoft VoIP portal</strong> provides access to phone number
  configuration, call center queue settings, emergency services setup, and other
  telephony features. To access the portal, you'll need the login credentials provided
  in your VoIP portal account creation email. Once logged in, you can manage your
  VoIP settings, configure call routing, and customize your telephony experience.
</p>
<p>
  This guide walks you through the login process and provides troubleshooting steps
  if you encounter issues accessing your account.
</p>
<hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">

<h3 id="h_01KKA24N7JPF6VGGXP4NZ1Z3GQ">Troubleshooting Login Issues</h3>
<div class="accordion">
  <p>
    <strong>I Didn't Receive My Login Credentials</strong>
  </p>
</div>
<div class="panel">
  <p>
    When your VoIP portal account is created, you will receive an email containing
    your login credentials. If you didn't receive this email or are unsure of
    your username:
  </p>
  <ol>
    <li data-list-item-id="e4a03d64ae754ab8b39e51662d6a22355">
      <p>
        Click <strong>Forgot your Password?</strong> on the login page.<br>
        <img src="/guide-media/01KKBZXQHNK47PS395Z14TJJ8W" width="502" height="202">
      </p>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
    </li>
    <li data-list-item-id="e7779e2a341f7398562d27b09b307aa84">
      <p>
        Enter your <strong>email address</strong> in the
        <strong>Identifier</strong> field.<br>
        <img src="/guide-media/01KKC01TXJ12MESYPD50F1QH3H" width="502" height="202">
      </p>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
    </li>
    <li data-list-item-id="ea6347887f892f409769c207d4461b2d2">
      <p>
        Click <strong>Next</strong>.<br>
        <img src="/guide-media/01KKC03K4YR032BB0QYP78SGFK" width="502" height="202">
      </p>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
      <p>
        Check your email for instructions to reset your password and log
        in.
      </p>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
    </li>
  </ol>
</div>
