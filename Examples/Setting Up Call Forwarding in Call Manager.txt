<p>
  <strong>Call Forwarding</strong> in Call Manager allows you to automatically
  redirect incoming calls to another phone number, such as a mobile phone, another
  extension, or an external line. This ensures you never miss important calls when
  you're away from your primary device. By configuring call forwarding rules, you
  can maintain seamless communication with customers and colleagues whether you're
  working remotely, on vacation, or simply away from your desk.
</p>
<p>
  Call Manager offers two methods for setting up call forwarding:
  <strong>Schedule-based forwarding</strong> for recurring patterns (such as after-hours
  or regular work schedules) and <strong>Status-based forwarding</strong> for temporary
  situations (such as vacations or short absences). This guide walks you through
  both approaches so you can choose the method that best fits your needs.
</p>

  <p>
    <strong>Best Practices</strong>
  </p>
</div>
<div class="panel">
  <ul>
    <li data-list-item-id="e4c8a66c449e1dbacd68ec7c339bb58b8">
      <p>
        Use <strong>Schedule-based forwarding</strong> for recurring patterns
        like after-hours or regular work schedules.
      </p>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
    </li>
    <li data-list-item-id="ec75a7a3bf88052b9e0bae752eecf1548">
      <p>
        Use <strong>Status-based forwarding</strong> for temporary situations
        like vacations or short absences.
      </p>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
    </li>
    <li data-list-item-id="e286a69e3b14d70e64414e5cca3d3ae35">
      <p>
        Always set your extension as the final destination to ensure voicemails
        are left in your mailbox, not the forwarded number's mailbox.
      </p>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
    </li>
    <li data-list-item-id="e392acdb79636e1787790337bd73bdfc1">
      <p>
        Use shorter ring times for the second destination (your extension)
        since the call has already attempted to reach you at the forwarded
        number.
      </p>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
    </li>
    <li data-list-item-id="edc66f1131a978b67be52940bc94c9c6e">
      Review and update your call forwarding rules regularly to ensure they
      remain accurate and relevant.
    </li>
  </ul>
