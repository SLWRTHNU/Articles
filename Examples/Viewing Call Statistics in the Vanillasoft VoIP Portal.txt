<p>
  <strong>Call Statistics</strong> reports provide a summary of call activity for
  your team and agents, including totals for inbound and outbound calls, call duration,
  and average time spent on calls. These reports help you analyze calling patterns,
  measure team productivity, and identify trends in call volume over specific date
  ranges. By reviewing call statistics regularly, you can make informed decisions
  about staffing, training, and resource allocation.
</p>
<p>
  Call statistics reports are generated through the Vanillasoft VoIP portal and
  can be printed or reviewed on-screen for further analysis.
</p>

  <p>
    <strong>Understanding Your Call Statistics Report</strong>
  </p>
</div>
<div class="panel">
  <p>
    The Call Statistics report displays the following information:
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li data-list-item-id="efce0b81609aa123eee761e07eeb15c72">
      <p>
        <strong>Inbound Calls</strong> – Total number of incoming calls received
        during the selected date range.
      </p>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
    </li>
    <li data-list-item-id="e210559116bfd93f491f9c9b2918e852b">
      <p>
        <strong>Outbound Calls</strong> – Total number of outgoing calls
        made during the selected date range.
      </p>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
    </li>
    <li data-list-item-id="e55f9be52463f3b238d4117e18d32e676">
      <p>
        <strong>Duration</strong> – Total time spent on calls, displayed
        for both inbound and outbound activity.
      </p>
      <hr style="height: 1px; visibility: hidden;">
    </li>
    <li data-list-item-id="eddd696d478fea77b51549352e0128d59">
      <strong>Average Call Time</strong> – Average duration of calls, helping
      you identify trends in call length and efficiency.
    </li>
  </ul>
