<p>
  <strong>Voicemail</strong> in <strong>Vanillasoft VoIP</strong> allows you to
  manage missed calls by storing messages from contacts who reach your voicemail.
  Configuring voicemail settings ensures that callers can leave messages when you're
  unavailable, and you can customize greetings, retention policies, and notification
  preferences to match your workflow. This feature is particularly useful for managing
  high call volumes, maintaining professionalism during off-hours, and ensuring
  no important message is missed.&nbsp;
</p>
<p>
  Voicemail settings are configured within the
  <strong>Vanillasoft VoIP portal</strong>. Once set up, voicemail messages are
  delivered to your extension, where you can retrieve and manage them based on
  your preferences.
</p>
