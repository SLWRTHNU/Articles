How to Edit an Extension's Call Recording Settings
This guide provides a straightforward process for editing call recording settings for extensions.

Click “Telephony”


Click on “Extensions”


Enter the Extension Number you want to review Call Recording settings for



Click the desired Extension Number


Click “System”


Click “Edit”

Click this checkbox to ENABLE Call Recording


Click “Save”


To DISABLE Call Recording for an Extension, from this screen click “Edit”



Uncheck this box by clicking on it


Once you see the box is Unchecked, click “Save”.
