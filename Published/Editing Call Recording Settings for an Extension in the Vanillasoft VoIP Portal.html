Call recording lets you capture inbound and outbound conversations for quality assurance, compliance, and training purposes. In the Vanillasoft VoIP portal, you can enable or disable call recording for individual extensions and configure when recordings are triggered.

Configuring call recording at the extension level gives you flexibility to record calls selectively, ensuring you capture the conversations most relevant to your team's needs.

Enable or Disable Call Recording for an Extension

  1. Log in to the Vanillasoft VoIP portal.

    • Note: This portal may display Cloudli branding because it is hosted by Cloudli. All configuration and support are handled by Vanillasoft. For assistance, contact Vanillasoft Support.

  2. In the left navigation menu, click Telephony, then select Extensions.

  3. Locate and click the extension you want to configure.

  4. Click Edit to open the extension settings.

  5. Click the Call Recording tab.

  6. Click Edit on the Call Recording tab to enable editing.

  7. Use the Record Inbound Calls and Record Outbound Calls toggles to enable or disable recording for each call direction.

    • Record Inbound Calls – captures calls received by this extension
    • Record Outbound Calls – captures calls placed from this extension

  8. (Optional) Configure the Recording Announcement setting to play an automated message notifying callers that the call is being recorded.

  9. Click Save to apply your call recording settings.

Call recordings configured at the extension level will be captured automatically and stored in the portal for review. To learn more about transferring and managing stored recordings, see Configuring Call Recording and Transferring Records in the Vanillasoft VoIP Portal. For additional assistance, contact Vanillasoft Support.
