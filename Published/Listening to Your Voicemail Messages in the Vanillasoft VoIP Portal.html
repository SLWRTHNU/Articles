This article explains how to access and listen to voicemail messages in the Vanillasoft VoIP portal. If you need to review messages left on a specific extension, the portal allows you to browse voicemail boxes by extension and download individual messages directly to your device for playback.

Listen to a Voicemail Message

  1. Log in to the Vanillasoft VoIP portal.

    • Note: This portal may display Cloudli branding because it is hosted by Cloudli. All configuration and support are handled by Vanillasoft. For assistance, contact Vanillasoft Support.

  2. In the left navigation menu, click Telephony, then select Voicemail.

  3. Locate and click the voicemail box for the extension you want to review.

  4. Click the Messages tab to view the list of voicemail messages associated with that extension.

  5. Click Download next to the message you want to listen to. The audio file will be saved to your device for playback.

For information on configuring voicemail options such as greetings and PIN settings, see Managing Your Voicemail Settings in the Vanillasoft VoIP Portal. If you need additional help accessing voicemail messages, contact Vanillasoft Support.
