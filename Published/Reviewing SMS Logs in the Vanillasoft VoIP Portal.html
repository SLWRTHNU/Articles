This article explains how to review and export SMS logs in the Vanillasoft VoIP portal. SMS logs allow you to view messaging activity for a specific phone number or user over a selected date range. You can also export the logs for record-keeping or reporting purposes.

Review and Export SMS Logs

  1. Log in to the Vanillasoft VoIP portal.

    • Note: This portal may display Cloudli branding because it is hosted by Cloudli. All configuration and support are handled by Vanillasoft. For assistance, contact Vanillasoft Support.

  2. In the left navigation menu, click Telephony, then select SMS Number.

  3. Use the search field to find the user or SMS number you want to review, then click the telephone number in the results list.

  4. Click the down arrow on the Menu Button to open the action dropdown.

  5. Click SMS Logs from the dropdown menu.

  6. Select the desired date range for the logs you want to view.

  7. Click Generate to load the SMS log results for the selected date range.

  8. Click Export to download the SMS logs to your device.

To review inbound and outbound call activity and usage statistics, see Viewing Call Statistics in the Vanillasoft VoIP Portal. If you need further assistance with SMS logs or reporting, contact Vanillasoft Support.
